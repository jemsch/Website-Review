    Youtube
    At one point in April 2018, Vevo was hacked. Two hackers replace some video clips of popular music videos such as Despacito, with violent images and videos with title changes too. These hackers were than arrested. This isn't the only case of hackings into youtube accounts. Many accounts have been hacked before as a result of only being protected by your gmail account and with the correct information, getting access to someones channel without permission is possible. Youtube is not an encrypted site. Any place you log in to from your gmail account that is not encrypted, can lead to exposed data and log in info. Youtube collects information from what you search and how you use Google search. They also have your contact information and can collect data on location. Your interactions on Google Search may lead to targeted ads. Youtube is safe and is a trusted sight, although connections to outside sources can be found in descriptions and ads which could potentially be dangerous. Descriptions and ads with links and download files could possibly contain a virus. For these reasons, I give Youtube a B in security.
    Rating: A+
